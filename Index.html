About Us

Our Benevolent Purpose

Chavivim Volunteer Emergency Services is a non-profit Public Benevolent Institution (PBI) dedicated to providing immediate, practical relief to individuals suffering from distress, helplessness, crisis, or severe misfortune. Guided by the values of chesed—selfless kindness and community welfare—our global network brings together hundreds of certified volunteers across multiple countries, bound by a singular mission: to step into moments of severe distress and restore safety, dignity, and relief to those facing adversity.

Relieving Distress and Hardship

At its core, Chavivim exists to support the community's most vulnerable during challenging situations. When an individual encounters an unexpected crisis—such as being stranded on a dangerous road due to a mechanical breakdown, locked out of their home during severe weather, or facing immediate displacement from local storm damage—they experience acute vulnerability and distress. Our highly structured operation provides a rapid, compassionate framework of scene management and practical aid to directly mitigate that suffering, operating alongside traditional emergency response services to ensure no one is left helpless in a time of crisis.

Core Programs

How We Deliver Benevolent Relief

📖

Public Awareness & Emergency Preparedness

One of our primary activities focusing on community-wide resilience and risk reduction before crises strike.

  • Community Risk Awareness Seminars
  • Emergency Preparedness Workshops
  • Severe Weather Safety Guidance
  • Public Safety Resources & Strategy
  • Vulnerable Group Prevention Support
🚨

Crisis Response & Relief Operations

Immediate direct intervention for larger-scale environmental and community emergencies causing hardship.

  • Storm Damage Relief & Scene Support
  • Flood Evacuation Assistance
  • Missing Persons Support (SAR)
  • Severe Misfortune Intervention
  • Coordinated Agency Collaboration
🛠️

Practical Hardship & Distress Relief

Targeted, physical actions designed purely to alleviate immediate trauma, helplessness, and individual misfortune.

  • Emergency Lockout Assistance (Home/Car)
  • Stranded Motorist Roadside Safety Support
  • Crisis Fuel and Battery Jump Starts
  • Vehicles Stuck in Hazardous Terrain
  • Direct On-Scene Practical Comfort

Coverage

Service Reach

Chavivim prioritises its relief work in the South-Eastern suburbs and Bayside areas of Melbourne, extending aid to surrounding regions to ensure no individual in distress is left beyond our reach. Our benevolent response teams operate 24 hours a day, 6 days a week.

South-Eastern Suburbs

Including Caulfield, Bentleigh, Moorabbin, and neighboring communities.

Bayside Areas

Including St Kilda, Brighton, Elwood, and all adjacent coastal communities.

We extend our care to surrounding areas! If you or someone you know is experiencing acute crisis or needs relief, Call us directly on 03-4233-2200.
