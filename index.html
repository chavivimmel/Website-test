Primary Pillar Activity

Community Risk Awareness & Safety Preparedness

Preventative Training & Resilience Building

Chavivim structures robust community awareness and safety educational programs across Melbourne to help mitigate crises before they emerge. By distributing vital weather-safety data, holding hazard-management information sessions, and tracking consumer digital privacy risks, we work proactively to reduce vulnerability and strengthen community resilience.

🔒 Smart Device & Dashcam Privacy Steps:

To secure your vehicle's integrated or aftermarket cloud dashcams from public visibility exposures: Disable default P2P cloud cloud-sharing configurations inside your camera's configuration application, change standard factory passwords immediately, and switch off default public streaming options under the system preferences menu.

Our Educational Initiatives Include:

  • • Environmental Hazard Briefings: Free public guides for secure property prep ahead of extreme flash floods or winter storm cycles.
  • • Roadside Safety Seminars: Practical guidance on vehicle health checkups, preventative maintenance, and basic defensive breakdown safety.
  • • Digital Privacy & Security Tracking: Educational instructions tracking shared software flaws to protect residents from unexpected tracking exposures.

Our Framework

Our Benevolent Purpose

Chavivim Volunteer Emergency Services is a registered non-profit Public Benevolent Institution (PBI) whose exclusive constitutional object is the relief of poverty, distress, helplessness, and misfortune. Our network of mobile volunteers steps into spaces of unexpected trauma and environmental disruption to deliver fast, practical, and completely free humanitarian comfort to anyone experiencing crisis.
